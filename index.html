Memory Lane: Greek shopping in 1950s-1970s Montréal Memory Lane maps show 22 selected Greek shops that existed between the years 1950 and 1980 in Montréal. The ads posted in Hellenic Canadian Tribune (VIMA) newspaper have been digitized and analyzed by MA student Stavroula Pabst and 22 shops have been selected by Postdoctoral fellow Dr. Alexandra Siotou based on the interviews conducted for the Immigrec Project. Based on the data provided, two maps have been created by Deniz Ozlem Cevik, research administrator for Immigrec at McGill.
